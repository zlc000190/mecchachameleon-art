# Contact

Contact us at [support@mecchachameleon.art](mailto:support@mecchachameleon.art).

You can use this address for:

- Factual corrections or outdated guide information.

- Copyright, trademark, or image-attribution concerns.

Please include the relevant page URL and a short description of the issue. We review legitimate correction and rights requests and update or remove material when appropriate.

This is an independent fan site. For purchases, account support, refunds, or technical support for the official MECCHA CHAMELEON PC game, contact the official publisher through Steam rather than this website.
