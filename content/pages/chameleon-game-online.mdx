## How the Game Works

In a chameleon game online, each player uses a paint tool to recolour their body to match the wall, floor, or prop behind them. The team with the best-painted Hiders wins, because a perfect colour match can hide you in plain sight.

A round typically lasts 2-3 minutes: a short paint phase, then a seeker hunt. The mechanics are identical to the full game - paint, crouch, match surfaces, freeze, and run.

## Why Paint Beats Hiding

Unlike regular hide and seek, where the dark corner wins, in this game the **best-painted corner** wins. The dark corner is the first place a Seeker looks. The painted corner is invisible.

The key is matching the **secondary colour**, not just the dominant one. A red brick wall has grey mortar lines. Match both, and the Seeker walks past. Match only the red, and the grey lines give you away.
