# About Meccha Chameleon Game Art

Meccha Chameleon Game Art is an independent, fan-made guide and browser-game directory. We publish original beginner explanations, map notes, camouflage observations, update summaries, and clearly attributed links to third-party browser games.

## Important relationship disclosure

This website is **not** operated by, affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by lemorion_1224, the developer and publisher of MECCHA CHAMELEON. The official MECCHA CHAMELEON is a paid Windows PC game distributed through [its official Steam page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4704690/MECCHA_CHAMELEON/).

Any browser game shown or linked on this site is a separate third-party or fan-made title. It is not the official Steam game. Provider names are displayed beside embedded games so readers can understand where each title comes from.

## What we publish

- Original gameplay explainers and beginner checklists.
- Map and camouflage notes written as community reference material.
- Clearly labeled links to official sources, including Steam and Steam Community.
- Browser-game recommendations with provider attribution.
- Corrections when readers identify inaccurate or outdated information.

## Editorial independence

We do not claim to speak for the game developer. Product names, screenshots, logos, and trademarks belong to their respective owners. References are used for identification, commentary, and community guide purposes.
