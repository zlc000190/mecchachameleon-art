## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Meccha Chameleon Game Art collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit this independent fan-made website. It also explains how advertising and analytics providers may use cookies or similar identifiers if those services are enabled in the future.

## Information We Collect

We may collect information in a few ways:

1. Information you provide directly, such as an email address or message sent to our support address.
2. Basic server logs used for security and reliability, such as IP address, request time, requested URL, user agent, and error information.
3. Information stored locally in your browser for convenience features, such as a cookie-choice flag or a locally saved crew list.
4. Usage, device, browser, and referral data if optional analytics is enabled with the required notice or consent.

We do not ask visitors to provide a legal name, home address, government identifier, or payment-card number through ordinary guide pages.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- operate and improve the website
- understand which pages are useful
- respond to messages or requests
- maintain security and prevent abuse

## Advertising and Google Services

AdSense ownership verification may use a site-level meta tag and an `ads.txt` record. Advertising scripts are disabled by default while the site is being reviewed.

If Google advertising is enabled after approval, Google and other third-party vendors may place or read cookies on your browser, or use web beacons, IP addresses, and other identifiers to serve, measure, and personalize ads. Google's use of information from sites that use its services is described at [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

Where required, we will use an appropriate consent mechanism before enabling personalized advertising or non-essential measurement. Visitors may also control cookies through browser settings and Google's advertising controls.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with hosting, security, analytics, advertising, payment, email, or technical-service providers only when the related feature is enabled. Third parties may process information under their own privacy policies. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect rights, users, and the service.

Embedded or linked browser games are operated by their named third-party providers. Opening or interacting with those games may send technical data to the provider. Review the provider's privacy information before using an embedded game.

## Data Retention and Security

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Your Choices

You can control cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Where the site presents a choice, selecting “Essential only” records that preference locally. If you contact us directly, you may request access, correction, or deletion of information we hold about you, subject to legal, security, and operational requirements.

## Children

This site is a general-audience game guide and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child submitted personal information, we will take reasonable steps to remove it. Interest-based advertising must not be enabled for child-directed activity.

## International Visitors

Visitors in the EEA, United Kingdom, Switzerland, California, and other regulated regions may have additional privacy rights. Optional personalized advertising and analytics will not be enabled in those regions without the notices, choices, and consent required by applicable law and Google policy.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date and post the updated version on this page.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at [support@mecchachameleon.art](mailto:support@mecchachameleon.art).
