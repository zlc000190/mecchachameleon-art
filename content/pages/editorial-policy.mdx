# Editorial Policy

Our goal is to publish useful, original guidance while making the source and status of every experience clear.

## Research and sourcing

We prefer first-party sources for facts about the official game, including the Steam store page, developer announcements, and official patch notes. Third-party reporting may provide context, but it does not replace official product information.

## Original value

Guide pages should add practical explanation, comparison, organization, or analysis. We do not intend to republish another site's article as our own. Embedded browser games are accompanied by provider attribution and original surrounding guidance.

## Official versus fan-made content

MECCHA CHAMELEON by lemorion_1224 is a paid Windows PC game. This website is an independent fan guide. Browser games on this site are separate third-party or fan-made experiences and must not be described as the official PC game.

## Corrections

We correct factual errors and outdated claims when identified. Send the page URL, disputed statement, and supporting source to [support@mecchachameleon.art](mailto:support@mecchachameleon.art).

## Advertising and commercial content

Advertising, if enabled after policy approval, must be visually distinguishable from editorial content. Advertising does not determine our guide conclusions. Paid products and affiliate relationships must be disclosed near the relevant link or offer.
